About

The genius is already in your people. I build the AI that frees it.

I started in people, not technology

For 18 years I led operations. I built the systems, hired the teams, and kept the work moving. I learned how a business really runs, which is to say I learned how much of it runs on one or two people holding everything together.

Then AI changed what was possible, and I saw it for what it actually is. Not a replacement for people. A way to take the weight off them. I watched talented people get buried under work a system could do, and I knew that was the problem worth solving.

So I build that system now. I take everything I learned about operations and I turn it into infrastructure that carries the load, so the people I work with get to do the part only they can do.

What I actually believe

AI gives people back to themselves. When the repetitive work runs on its own, your team stops being a set of hands and goes back to being the reason your business is good. That is the whole point. The technology is in service of the people, never the other way around.

What I bring to the table

18+ years in operations

Real leadership experience running teams and systems, not theory. I have sat in the chair you are sitting in and I know what the work costs.

Certified project manager

The discipline to scope a build, run it cleanly, and deliver it on a timeline you can count on.

Working Genius facilitator

A framework for finding where each person does their best work, so the systems I build fit your team's real strengths.

A2A build methodology

My approach to building AI infrastructure that connects to how your business actually works and hands cleanly to your team.

Based in Denver, working across the country

I am in Denver, Colorado, and I work with founders and teams all over the United States. Every engagement runs remotely with live working sessions, so where you are has never been the thing that matters.
